A Music Bio

CubeOfCheese is a flourishing music duo from Sacramento, CA comprised of Billy T. Hernandez and Cubeo F. Cheese, who share a psychological connection. Cubeo’s creative powers combined with Billy T.’s opposable thumbs (good for playing instruments) results in a savory and slightly electronic sound. Assuming Billy T. doesn’t get too hungry, CubeOfCheese will continue aging to perfection. It is no doubt that their layered lyricism and tasty bass riffs will soon become the desire of even the most discriminating palates.
